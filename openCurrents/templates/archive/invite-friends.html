Help me introduce openCurrents to...
  • an individual

    openCurrents is an alternative currency. Anyone can create and earn 1 Current for each hour donated to a nonprofit or social business.

    1 Current has a suggested initial value of $10. The website will connect you to businesses who accept Currents for a discount on your purchase.

    You can also earn Currents by inviting individuals or organizations to participate.

  • a social organization

    openCurrents is an alternative currency. Volunteers create and earn 1 Current for each hour donated to a nonprofit or social business.

    This means that it's free to pay volunteers with Currents. You just need to approve hours recorded by volunteers once a week and abide by community ground rules.

    1 Current has a suggested initial value of $10. The website will connect volunteers with businesses who accept Currents for a discount.

  • a business

    openCurrents is an alternative currency that allows anyone to receive 1 Current for each hour donated to a nonprofit or social business.

    Currents will gain value as more people use them. Businesses accepting Currents will be able to use them or sell them at a later date for a profit.

    1 Current has a suggested inital value of $10. Businesses who agree to accept Currents will be promoted to their target audience.
