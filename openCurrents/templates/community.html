2017-01-21 13:00:00 2017-01-21 15:00:00 America/Chicago openCurrents Community Kickoff Meeting Help us choose the direction of the openCurrents alternative currency project. If you are outside Austin, email danny@opencurrents.com to join by video call. 1101 Emmitt Run, Austin, TX 78721 Danny Pernik danny@opencurrents.com

openCurrents is a community-led currency. Come help us determine the direction of this project. You'll get paid 2 Currents for your time - these will be the first Currents to exist!

We will start with an introduction to openCurrents and alternative currency, followed by Q&A. We will then vote to answer open questions about how to implement the currency.
